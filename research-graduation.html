2026/04/01

Graduation research project started

I started my graduation research project on the mineralogical relationship between dolomite and clay minerals in the Orgueil CI chondrite. The project focuses on whether dolomite in primitive carbonaceous chondrites is spatially, chemically, and crystallographically associated with clay minerals, and whether such relationships may provide insight into the so-called “Dolomite problem.”

The initial research plan is to investigate the occurrence, morphology, chemical composition, and crystallographic features of dolomite, especially near clay-rich regions. The planned analytical approach includes SEM/EPMA observations at the micrometre scale, single-grain XRD analysis for crystallographic information, and TEM observations at the nanometre scale.
