Mission Log Safe Liquid Glass Restore

Goal:
- Restore frosted/liquid glass feeling and high-light language in Mission Log.
- Keep the Edge/Chromium vertical band bug fixed.

What changed:
1. Status cards, mission record fields, mission log cards, tags, pills, badges, and secondary buttons get glass back.
2. Backdrop blur is restored, but surfaces are made more opaque so the asteroid/grid background cannot cut through as vertical bands.
3. Full-height lateral/diagonal highlight gradients are not restored. Those were the risky part.
4. Highlights are now local:
   - soft top-edge glint
   - corner radial specular glow
   - thin border refraction
5. Hover states keep liquid response without moving a large internal gradient.

Design principle:
- Preserve the previous Interface 2046 liquid-glass look.
- Remove only the bug-causing geometry: wide vertical/lateral highlight bands and overly transparent surfaces.
