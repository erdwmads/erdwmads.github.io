About

I am interested in how primitive extraterrestrial materials preserve mineralogical and chemical records of the early Solar System. My current academic focus lies between mineralogy, cosmochemistry, and planetary materials science.

Research

Primitive Solar System materials

I am interested in carbonaceous chondrites and asteroid-returned samples, especially materials that record aqueous alteration and low-temperature mineral reactions on parent bodies.

Ryugu and Bennu

Ryugu and Bennu samples provide direct records of primitive asteroid materials and are important for comparing meteorites with returned samples under clean analytical conditions.

Electron microscopy and microanalysis

I am interested in connecting microtextures, chemistry, and crystallography using SEM, EPMA, TEM, EDS, EELS, EBSD, XRD, and related analytical methods.
