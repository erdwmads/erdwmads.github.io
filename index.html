Current Focus

Dolomite and clay minerals in Orgueil CI chondrite

My current graduation research investigates whether dolomite in the Orgueil CI chondrite is spatially, chemically, and crystallographically associated with clay minerals. I am interested in whether clay-rich surfaces may have acted as sites for carbonate nucleation and whether this relationship can provide an astromaterials perspective on the “Dolomite problem.”

Current question Is dolomite genetically related to clay-mineral surfaces?
Target material Orgueil CI chondrite
Methods SEM/EPMA · single-grain XRD · TEM
Broader context Carbonate formation, aqueous alteration, and early Solar System materials
