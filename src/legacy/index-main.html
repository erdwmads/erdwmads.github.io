Current Focus

Cosmomineralogical Study of Dolomite in the Orgueil CI1 Chondrite: Aqueous Alteration Processes and Material Evolution in a Primitive Asteroidal Parent Body

My current graduation research investigates dolomite in the Orgueil CI1 chondrite as a mineralogical record of primitive chondritic constituent formation. The project focuses on the morphology, chemical composition, crystallographic characteristics, and spatial relation between dolomite and clay minerals, aiming to constrain formation conditions from micro- to nanometre scales.

Current question What conditions controlled dolomite formation in the Orgueil CI1 chondrite?
Target material Orgueil CI1 chondrite
Methods SEM/EPMA · single-grain XRD · TEM
Broader context Primitive chondritic constituents, aqueous alteration, and the Dolomite problem
