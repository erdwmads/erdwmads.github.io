Research

宇宙礦物學

我的主要研究興趣是 宇宙礦物學: the study of minerals and mineralogical processes in extraterrestrial materials, especially primitive Solar System materials.

主要興趣

宇宙礦物學

I am interested in how minerals in meteorites and asteroid-returned samples preserve information about nebular condensation, parent-body aqueous alteration, low-temperature reactions, and the early evolution of the Solar System.

畢業研究

Orgueil CI1 球粒隕石中白雲石的礦物學研究：制約原始球粒隕石構成物質的形成條件

My graduation research focuses on dolomite in the Orgueil CI1 球粒隕石, aiming to constrain the formation conditions of primitive chondritic constituents through mineralogical, chemical, and crystallographic observations. The project connects carbonate formation, clay-mineral surfaces, aqueous alteration, and the broader 白雲石問題.

主題

碳質球粒隕石

Primitive meteorites preserve mineralogical and chemical records of early Solar System environments and parent-body processes.

Ryugu 與 Bennu

Asteroid-returned samples provide clean reference materials for comparing primitive asteroids with meteorite collections.

電子顯微鏡與微區分析

I am interested in connecting texture, chemistry, and crystallography using SEM, EPMA, TEM, EDS, EELS, EBSD, XRD, and related analytical methods.

分析路徑

從目標顆粒到形成模型

01樣品選擇

選定合適的 Orgueil CI1 材料與含碳酸鹽區域。

02SEM / BSE 觀察

記錄形態、組織，以及與富黏土區域的空間關係。

03EPMA 元素映射

制約元素分布與碳酸鹽化學。

04單顆粒 XRD

研究結晶學特徵與碳酸鹽有序化。

05TEM / EDS / 繞射

解析奈米尺度界面、缺陷與微構造制約。

06形成條件模型

整合觀察結果，討論原始球粒隕石構成物質的形成。
