目前研究焦點

Orgueil CI1 球粒隕石中白雲石的礦物學研究：制約原始球粒隕石構成物質的形成條件

My current graduation research investigates dolomite in the Orgueil CI1 球粒隕石 as a mineralogical record of primitive chondritic constituent formation. The project focuses on the morphology, chemical composition, crystallographic characteristics, and spatial relation between dolomite and clay minerals, aiming to constrain formation conditions from micro- to nanometre scales.

當前問題 Orgueil CI1 球粒隕石中的白雲石形成受到哪些條件控制？

方法 SEM/EPMA · 單顆粒 XRD · TEM
